Top 3 Places to visit in 2023

Darjeeling

Darjeeling, a Himalayan gem, entices travelers with its stunning tea-covered slopes, awe-inspiring Himalayan vistas, and a blend of colonial and local charm, making it an enchanting destination for those seeking serene beauty and cultural exploration.

Sikkim

Sikkim, a paradise in the mountains, offers travelers a captivating blend of lush landscapes, diverse flora and fauna, rich Buddhist heritage, and warm local hospitality, making it an ideal destination for a soul-enriching and immersive travel experience.

Kalimpong

Nestled in the Himalayan embrace, Kalimpong beckons travelers with its tranquil ambiance, panoramic mountain views, and a tapestry of cultures that come alive through monasteries, flower nurseries, and local markets, offering a serene and culturally enriching escape.
